Lasagna

Description

A yummy lasagna that feeds as many as the ingrediants you pick would.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Itailian Spices

Steps

  1. Cook The Noodles
  2. Cook The Beef
  3. Preheat the oven to 400.
  4. Mix itialian seasoning with the beef.
  5. Layer Ingredients like you think they should be.
  6. Top with extra amounts of cheese.
  7. Bake in the oven until it looks like a lasagna.
