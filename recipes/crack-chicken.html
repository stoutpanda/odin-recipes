Crack Chicken/h1>

Description

A yummy keto recipe I can't wait to try.

Ingredients

  • Canned Chicken
  • Ranch Seasoning
  • Cream Cheese
  • Mixed Shredded Cheese
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. Precook the broccoli if frozen
  3. Mix the chicken, cream cheese and ranch together.
  4. Place chicken and broccoli into pan. Spread out.
  5. Top with extra cheese!
  6. Heat until yummy looking. Probably 20 minutes.
